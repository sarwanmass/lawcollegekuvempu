Mandatory Certificate Checklist

Candidates must submit original physical transcripts and certificates of the following credentials during the counseling desk verification phase:

  • SSLC / 10th Cumulative Marks Card

    Proof of birth date and secondary school clearance.

  • PUC / 12th Cumulative Marks Card

    Senior secondary certificates and transcripts.

  • Degree Graduation Cumulative Transcripts

    Original degree certificate and semester marks cards.

  • Original Transfer Certificate (TC)

    From the last attended university/institution.

  • Original Migration Certificate

    Mandatory only for Non-Karnataka Graduation pathways.

  • Recent Passport Size Photos (8 Copies)

    Clear color photos with white or blue background.

Curriculum Highlights

A rigorous blend of procedural laws, substantial codes, and clinical programs that builds practical legal capabilities:

Foundational Jurisprudence

Constitutional Law, Law of Torts, Indian Penal Code (IPC), Family Law and Contract Laws form the academic bedrock.

Procedural Competence

Civil Procedure Code (CPC), Criminal Procedure Code (CrPC), Indian Evidence Act, and Draftings are thoroughly explored.

Professional Excellence & Clinicals

Four mandatory clinical papers including Moot Court exercises, Professional Ethics, ADR, and Legal Aid services.
