Curriculum

KSLU 3-Year LLB Core Syllabus Finalised

May 12, 2026

The academic board has published the curriculum mapping for the 3-Year professional LLB course spanning 6 semesters.

Click to view semester structures

The curriculum spans three functional academic years split into two semesters of 18 continuous teaching weeks apiece:

  • Semester I: Constitutional Law I, Law of Torts, Law of Contracts I, Family Law I.
  • Semester II: Constitutional Law II, Family Law II, Indian Penal Code (Criminal Law), Law of Contracts II.
  • Semester III: Jurisprudence, Property Law, Labour Law I, Administrative Law.
  • Semester IV: Public International Law, Environmental Law, Labour Law II, Banking Law.
  • Semester V: Law of Evidence, Civil Procedure Code (CPC), Intellectual Property Rights (IPR), Clinical Paper I.
  • Semester VI: Law of Taxation, Company Law, Drafting & Pleading (Clinical Paper IV), Moot Court Hall Advocacy.
