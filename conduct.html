Campus Compliance

Directives & Ethical Standards

To foster academic focus and build upright constitutional advocates, students must strictly adhere to the following 12 guidelines.

01

No Student Elections Strict Ban

Political activity, external canvassing, strikes, or student union election campaigns are strictly prohibited on campus to ensure academic peace, focus, and decorum.

02

75% Attendance Mandatory

Under KSLU guidelines, students must maintain a minimum of 75% attendance in both academic lectures and practical clinical papers to qualify for university examinations.

03

Campus Decorum & Silence

Shouting, loitering in corridors, and acts of campus vandalism are strictly penalized. Students are expected to maintain professional decorum and treat college property with respect.

04

Zero Exam Malpractice Expulsion

Any form of copying, cheating, smuggling handwritten slips, using mobile phones in tests, or plagiarizing journals will result in immediate suspension and KSLU tribunal reporting.

05

Mandatory ID Cards Security

Students must wear their official college ID cards around their neck at all times. Security personnel are authorized to deny classroom or library access to students without badges.

06

Monitor Notice Boards

It is the student's primary responsibility to review physical notice boards and official digital communications daily for critical circulars, exam timings, and administrative changes.

07

Library Protocols

Absolute silence is mandatory inside the Majestic Law Library. Borrowed books must be returned by due dates. Damaged or lost volumes must be replaced or paid for in full.

08

Authorized Tours Only

Educational tours, picnics, or legal aid field camps must be officially sanctioned by the Principal. Written parent/guardian consent is mandatory for participation.

09

Parental Engagement

Parents and guardians are required to attend the college and interface with the faculty or Principal immediately upon receipt of official notifications regarding student progress.

10

Respect for Authority

RKLC demands exemplary courtesy. Insolent, discourteous, or abusive behavior towards teaching professors, guest advocates, or registry clerks is subject to immediate disciplinary actions.

11

Substance Abuse Ban Criminal Charge

Possessing, consuming, or distributing tobacco, vapes, alcohol, or illicit narcotics within college premises is strictly banned. Violations trigger police handover and academic expulsion.

12

Formal Legal Attire Uniform

Students must strictly wear the official legal uniform: crisp white shirts, formal black trousers, black blazers/coats, and polished black formal shoes, emphasizing litigation readiness.
